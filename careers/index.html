Why work for us?

A bit about orchestrated

We began our journey in 2014 for the purpose of empowering large corporations with the tools and processes needed to affect digital transformation, and since then have expanded to provide multiple services across Melbourne and Sydney.

We are a young growing company with an exciting future with an empowered collaborative culture. We are looking to grow to be able to expand our offerings even further and increase the speed at which we can deliver meaningful value to our clients. We are a bunch of passionate and talented individuals and so we are looking for more of the same to help us in this endeavour.

Our culture

We really value our employees. And this isn't just marketing talk, the proof is in the pudding; regular (work-paid) outings revolving around food, coffee or a cold brew are a common occurance (we are foodies and like to be social about it), quarterly offsite workshops for the whole company, fun social events, christmas parties, conferences, talks, the works. And because we care about work life balance, we are flexible with working hours, every staff is given the autonomy to manage their own time and be responsible and acountable for their own work; we treat you like adults, not children telling you what you can and can't do.

Our culture is fun and relaxed, but we know our business, and are self driven and motivated to conquer any challenge and succeed. Everyone is accountable and responsible for their own deliverables. Collaboration and knowledge sharing is encouraged, not just within our company, but in working with our clients as well.

OUR KEY VALUES

  • Collaborate to continuously deliver customer value
  • Passionate about software engineering and digital transformation
  • Continually learn and share your learnings
  • Work without blame and challenge the status quo
  • Choose facetime first

As a whole, we embrace these values, and are continuously looking for people who could see themselves as a fit for these values and mindset.

Perks

Alongside attractive salaries, we offer additional benefits including:

Really want that new Apple iPhone, Samsung Galaxy or Google Pixel? We reimburse every employee up to $80 per month to cover their phone and internet bill.

$2000 and 4 days every 12 months dedicated to every employee for training purposes; whether that be doing a course, going to a conference or participating in a workshop or hackathon; whatever floats your boat.

Are you a Mac or Windows fan? Either way, your choice of hardware and software that you require to be comfortable and productive in the way you like working. Keep your wallet in your pocket, we'll cover it.

Who are we looking for?

Our idea candidate would likely be someone who has the following qualities:

  • Leader of business change, trainer, mentor or conference speaker. Agile evangelist and practitioner.
  • Ability to identify and challenge the status-quo with people, processes & technology, and sensitively implement change whilst continuing to deliver value to our customers.
  • Exceptional understanding of transforming enterprise technology delivery from traditional to modern software delivery.
  • Excellent communication skills in technical and business environments. Calm under pressure. Humble.
  • Contributor to the professional development of colleagues through sharing knowledge, teaching, and learning from others.

Experience history isn't everything

Although the jobs we are listing are for senior positions, if you fit our company values and qualities above, yet don't quite have the experience to apply for a senior role, we'd still love to hear from you. We are also seeking ambitious, intelligent people who want to learn and grow their career. So, if this sounds like you, please still reach out to us below.
